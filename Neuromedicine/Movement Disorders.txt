Movement Disorders
Which of the following signs is considered the CARDINAL sign of Parkinson's disease?
A) Bradykinesia
B) Resting tremor
C) Spasticity
D) Loss of smell
Correct Answer: A) Bradykinesia
###
What is the most appropriate diagnosis?
A) Parkinson's disease
B) Essential tremor
C) Wilson's disease
D) Drug-induced tremor
Correct Answer: B) Essential tremor
###
What is the most appropriate treatment for Parkinson's disease?
A) Methotrexate
B) Levodopa and a decarboxylase inhibitor (e.g., Levodopa/Carbidopa)
C) Propranolol
D) Penicillamine
Correct Answer: B) Levodopa and a decarboxylase inhibitor (e.g., Levodopa/Carbidopa)
